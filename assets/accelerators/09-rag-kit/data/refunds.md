# Refund & Returns Policy (sample)

## Refund policy
Customers may request a refund within 30 days of purchase. Refunds are issued to the
original payment method within 5 to 7 business days of approval. Items must be
unused and in their original packaging to qualify.

## Exceptions
Digital products and personalized items are non-refundable once delivered, except
where required by law. Shipping fees are not refundable.

## How to request
Submit a refund request through the support portal with your order number. Our team
reviews requests within 2 business days.
