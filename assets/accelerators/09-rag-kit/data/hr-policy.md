# HR Policy Handbook (sample)

## Paid time off (PTO)
New employees receive 15 days of paid time off per year, accrued monthly. PTO must
be requested at least one week in advance through the HR portal. Unused PTO of up to
5 days may be carried into the following year.

## Working hours
Standard hours are 9:00 to 17:00, Monday to Friday. Flexible start times between
7:00 and 10:00 are permitted with manager approval.

## Parental leave
Eligible employees are entitled to 12 weeks of paid parental leave after one year of
continuous service.
