# Loom walkthrough script (~90 seconds)

Record screen + voice. Goal: the client sees a real, modern intranet they could
sign off on today — before any tenant work. Keep it calm and concrete.

---

**0:00 — Intro (10s)**
> "Hi <name> — a quick look at the intranet I'm proposing for you. Everything you'll
> see is generated from one blueprint, and you can click through it right now,
> before we build anything in your tenant."

**0:10 — The home page (20s)**
> "This is the home page: a branded hero, your latest company news as cards, and
> quick links to the sections that matter — HR, IT, Policies. One front door for the
> whole company instead of email and scattered drives."

**0:30 — The sections (20s)**
> "Top navigation takes you to each section. Here's HR — onboarding, benefits, the
> org chart. IT has how-tos and service requests. Policies holds your controlled
> documents. Each one is its own area with its own permissions."

**0:50 — The Document Center (25s)**
> "This is the part that fixes 'where's the document?'. Every library has real
> metadata — department, owner, review date, status — so you *filter* to find a
> file instead of digging through folders. Look: I can see every policy due for
> review and who owns it, at a glance."

**1:15 — Governance + wrap (15s)**
> "Behind this is a naming, permissions, and retention model so it stays clean as
> it grows. If this structure looks right, I tailor the blueprint to your exact
> sections and build it in your tenant. Tell me what to change and I'll regenerate
> this preview the same day. Thanks <name>."

---

**Tips:** open `out/index.html` from the generator before recording; click through
Home → a section → Document Center so the navigation is visible; pause while pages
load; put the Loom link at the top of your proposal.
