# Information Security Policy (sample)

## Reporting a security incident
If you suspect a security incident — a lost device, a phishing email, or
unauthorized access — contact the security team immediately at security@example.com
or call the 24/7 hotline. Do not attempt to investigate on your own.

## Passwords and MFA
All accounts must use multi-factor authentication. Passwords must be at least 14
characters and are rotated every 12 months.

## Data handling
Confidential data must not be stored on personal devices or shared with external
parties without approval. Client data is never used to train public AI models.
