# Loom walkthrough script (3–5 min)

Record screen + voice. Keep it short and calm — the goal is the client feeling
"I can manage this." Fewer than 10% of freelancers send a video; it's a review magnet.

---

**0:00 — Intro (15s)**
> "Hi <name>, this is a quick walkthrough of the <solution> I built for you. I'll
> show you what it does, how to check it's working, and how to make small changes.
> The written guide covers everything too."

**0:15 — What it does (45s)**
> "At a high level: every <day>, it <pulls X from Y>, <transforms it>, and <writes
> it to Z>, then <notifies you>. Here's the result — <show the output: the
> SharePoint table / dashboard>."

**1:00 — Where it lives + how to check it's healthy (90s)**
> "This is the flow, here in Power Automate. This is the run history — green means
> it ran fine. If anything fails, you'll get an email here, and you can click into
> the run to see exactly which step had a problem."

**2:30 — Making a common change (60s)**
> "Say you want to <add a recipient / change the schedule>. You open <step>, and
> <do the edit>, then Save. That's it. The runbook lists the other common changes."

**3:30 — Wrap (20s)**
> "That's the whole thing. The handover guide has every detail and my contact for
> the <support window>. Thanks <name> — it was a pleasure building this."

---

**Tips:** rehearse once; pause recording while you navigate; trim dead air; put the
link at the top of the handover guide.
