# IT Support FAQ (sample)

## Resetting your password
You can reset your password from the self-service portal at portal.example.com.
Click "Forgot password" and follow the email link. Resets take effect immediately.

## VPN access
To connect to the VPN, install the company VPN client from the software center and
sign in with your work account. Contact IT support if your access is denied.

## Requesting new software
Submit a software request through the IT portal. Approvals usually take 1-2 business
days. Unapproved software may not be installed on company devices.
